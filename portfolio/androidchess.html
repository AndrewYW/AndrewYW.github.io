Background

Android Chess was the final project of my Software Methodology class. The premise was to port over a previously completed project, Ascii Chess, to a Google Nexus phone, running Android OS. Additionally, two new features would also be added: Saving completed games (i.e: a list of moves made), and the option to play previously completed games turn by turn.
